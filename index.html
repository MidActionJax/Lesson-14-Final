What is Music Therapy?

Music therapy is an evidence-based healthcare profession that uses music interventions to improve a client's physical, emotional, and cognitive health. A credentialed music therapist will tailor a treatment plan using various music techniques, such as listening to music, playing instruments, singing, and songwriting, to address specific needs and goals.

Benefits of Music Therapy

Here is some of the many benefits of music therapy:

  • Reduce stress and anxiety
  • Improve mood and emotional regulation
  • Manage pain
  • Enhance cognitive function
  • Promote communication and social interaction
  • Support physical rehabilitation
